Privacy Policy

This policy explains how TutorsOnline.pk handles accounts, tutor documents, payments, and live-session information.

What we collect

Name, email, city, profile details, uploaded qualification documents, tutor demo links, payment notices, booking records, and platform activity needed to run tutoring services.

How we use it

To verify tutors, match students with tutors, process manual credit purchases, confirm bookings, improve platform safety, and communicate account or session updates.

Tutor verification

Tutor documents and demo materials may be reviewed by the admin team for authenticity and quality assurance.

Payments and notices

Manual credit purchases and tutor activation fee notices may be stored for review, approval, decline, reconciliation, and audit trail purposes.

Safety and moderation

Chats, live sessions, and platform actions may be monitored where needed for policy enforcement, dispute review, and quality assurance.

Student and tutor visibility

Only approved, public, and verified tutors should appear publicly on the platform.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact superadmin@tutorsonline.pk.
